school excurtions

Trip to Nainital
Visited Naini Lake , Naina devi Temple, Shooting Activity, D.J. Night

Surrounded by panoramic seven hills. It is one of the most visited lakes in India. Naini Devi Temple is sacred place of great devotion in Nainital. Goddess Naini Devi is the diety of Naini Devi Temple and located at northern end of famous Nainital Lake. Goddess in this holy temple is represented by her two eyes. Devotees from far away place comes here to take blessing of Maa Naina Devi.

Trip to Japan
Visited several places in Japan that are very amazing.

Senior students visited places at Japan along with our principal and teachers. At Japan's Yamashita Pier, about 25 miles south of Tokyo, the world's largest humanoid robot has taken shape. They learned Japanese language, visited labs, did team work and many more.

Trip to Orlando U.S.A
Visited N.A.S.A

Senior students visited places at Orland along with our principal and teachers. NASA stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration. NASA was started on October 1, 1958, as a part of the United States government. NASA is in charge of U.S. science and technology that has to do with airplanes or space.
